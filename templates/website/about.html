About

This page was built by IS Jr Core Students for IS Jr Core Students.

Throughout their Jr. Core experience, the creators noticed a significant lack of resources on specific online topics related to each of their classes. This was made increasingly obvious as the creators attempted to help other Jr. Core teams with difficult assignments.

Many times over the course of the semester other teams asked the creators to point them to online resources. Each time they were asked, the creators would scour the internet for good resources and come up empty-handed. Eventually, they just started explaining the difficult concepts in their own words to anyone who asked.
